## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click the **Sign Up** button in the top navigation bar
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Verify your email address if prompted
4. Complete your profile information (optional but recommended)

### Signing In

1. Click the **Sign In** button in the top navigation bar
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. You'll be redirected to the homepage upon successful login

> [!TIP]
> Stay signed in to enjoy a personalized shopping experience, track your orders, and save items to your wishlist!

## Browsing Products

### Using the Shop Page

Access the shop page by clicking **Shop** in the navigation bar. Here you can:

#### Search Products
- Use the **search bar** at the top to find specific products
- Enter product names, brands, or keywords

#### Filter Products
Use the filter panel to narrow down your search:

- **Categories**: Filter by product type (Kitchen Appliances, Televisions, Desktop PC, Retro Tech, Tablets, Audio, Home Appliances)
- **Brands**: Filter by manufacturer
- **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum price limits
- **Sort By**: Order products by:
  - Relevance (default)
  - Price: Low to High
  - Price: High to Low
  - Newest First
  - Name: A-Z
  - Name: Z-A

#### Reset Filters
Click the **Reset Filters** button to clear all active filters and start fresh.

### Product Details Page

Click on any product to view its details:

- **Product Images**: View high-quality images of the product
- **Price**: Current price and any applicable discounts
- **Description**: Detailed product information
- **Specifications**: Technical details and features
- **Stock Status**: Availability information
- **Reviews**: Read customer reviews and ratings
- **Related Products**: See similar items you might like

### Product Actions

From the product page, you can:
- **Add to Cart**: Add the item to your shopping cart
- **Add to Wishlist**: Save the item for later
- **Share**: Share the product with friends (if available)

## Using the Shopping Cart

### Adding Items to Cart

Add items to your cart by:
1. Clicking the **Add to Cart** button on any product card
2. Clicking **Add to Cart** on a product detail page
3. Specifying quantity before adding (if available)

### Viewing Your Cart

Access your cart by:
- Clicking the **cart icon** in the navigation bar
- The cart icon shows the number of items currently in your cart

### Cart Management

In your cart, you can:

#### Adjust Quantities
- Use the **+** and **-** buttons to increase or decrease item quantities
- The subtotal updates automatically

#### Remove Items
- Click the **remove** or **trash** icon to delete items from your cart

#### Clear Cart
- Use the **Reset Cart** option to remove all items at once

### Cart Summary

Your cart displays:
- **Subtotal**: Total cost of items before shipping
- **Item Count**: Number of products in cart
- **Product Images**: Thumbnail images for easy identification

### Proceeding to Checkout

When ready to purchase:
1. Review your cart items
2. Click **Proceed to Checkout** or **Checkout** button
3. You'll be directed to the checkout page

> [!NOTE]
> You must be signed in to proceed to checkout.

## Managing Your Wishlist

### What is the Wishlist?

The wishlist allows you to save products you're interested in for future purchase. Items remain in your wishlist until you remove them or add them to your cart.

### Adding to Wishlist

Save items by:
- Clicking the **heart icon** on product cards
- Clicking **Add to Wishlist** on product detail pages

### Viewing Your Wishlist

Access your wishlist by:
- Clicking the **heart icon** in the navigation bar
- Going to **Account > Wishlist**

### Wishlist Actions

From your wishlist, you can:
- **Add to Cart**: Move items to your shopping cart
- **Remove**: Delete items from your wishlist
- **Clear All**: Remove all items at once
- **View Details**: Click on products to see full details

> [!TIP]
> Use the wishlist to save items during sales or to compare products before making a purchase decision!

## Account & Profile Management

### Accessing Your Account

Navigate to your account by:
1. Clicking on your **profile icon** in the navigation bar
2. Selecting **Profile** or **Account** from the dropdown menu

### Profile Information

Your profile contains:
- **Personal Details**: Name, email, phone number
- **Profile Picture**: Your account image
- **Date of Birth**: Optional personal information
- **Account Status**: Active/inactive status
- **Loyalty Points**: Rewards earned from purchases

### Updating Your Profile

To edit your profile:
1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Click the **Edit Profile** button
3. Update your information in the sidebar form
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Managing Addresses

#### Adding a New Address
1. Navigate to **Profile > Addresses**
2. Click **Add Address**
3. Fill in the address form:
   - Full Name
   - Street Address
   - City
   - State/Province
   - ZIP/Postal Code
   - Country
   - Phone Number
   - Address Type (Home/Office/Other)
4. Toggle **Set as Default** if desired
5. Click **Save Address**

#### Editing an Address
1. Find the address in your profile
2. Click the **Edit** icon
3. Make your changes
4. Save the updated address

#### Setting Default Address
- Mark an address as **default** for faster checkout
- Your default address will be pre-selected during checkout

#### Deleting an Address
1. Click the **delete** icon on the address you want to remove
2. Confirm the deletion

> [!IMPORTANT]
> You cannot delete an address that is currently set as default. Please set another address as default first.

## Checkout & Payments

### Checkout Process

1. **Review Cart**: Verify items and quantities
2. **Select Address**: Choose or add a shipping address
3. **Review Order**: Confirm order details and total
4. **Select Payment**: Choose your payment method
5. **Complete Payment**: Finalize your purchase

### Selecting a Shipping Address

During checkout:
- **Select an existing address** from your saved addresses
- **Add a new address** if needed
- Your **default address** is pre-selected for convenience

### Payment Methods

EcoTech Solutions accepts the following payment methods:

#### Stripe (Credit/Debit Cards)
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Other major credit/debit cards

#### Pay on Delivery
- Place your order and pay when you receive your items
- Available for select locations

### Payment Security

> [!NOTE]
> All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.

### Order Confirmation

After completing your purchase:
1. You'll see an **order confirmation page**
2. A **confirmation email** will be sent to your registered email
3. You can view your order in the **Orders** section

## Order Tracking & History

### Viewing Your Orders

Access your orders by:
1. Going to **Account > Orders**
2. Clicking **My Orders** from the navigation menu

### Order Information

Each order displays:
- **Order Number**: Unique identifier for your order
- **Order Date**: When the order was placed
- **Order Status**: Current status of your order
- **Payment Status**: Payment confirmation status
- **Total Amount**: Final order value (in KSh)
- **Products**: Items included in the order

### Order Statuses

Your order can have the following statuses:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Order received, awaiting processing |
| **Processing** | Order is being prepared |
| **Shipped** | Order has been dispatched |
| **Out for Delivery** | Order is on the way |
| **Delivered** | Order has been delivered |
| **Cancelled** | Order has been cancelled |

### Order Actions

From your orders page, you can:
- **View Details**: See complete order information
- **Download Invoice**: Generate a PDF invoice for your order
- **Pay Now**: Complete payment for unpaid orders
- **Track Order**: View order timeline and tracking information

### Order Timeline

The order timeline shows:
- Order placement date and time
- Processing updates
- Shipping information
- Delivery confirmation
